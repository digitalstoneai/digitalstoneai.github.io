FAQ

Questions clients and teams usually ask first

A straight read on how I work, what I build, and where DigitalStoneAI Studio fits into practical AI projects.

What kind of AI systems do you build?

I build practical AI automation, agent workflows, RAG systems, intake tools, reporting dashboards, inspection support systems, and AI media workflows. The goal is not hype. The goal is a tool that helps a real person move faster, see clearer, or make a better decision.

Do you work with companies, clients, or hiring teams?

Yes. I am open to company roles, contract builds, consulting work, and client projects. If the work sits at the edge of AI, operations, automation, creative production, or technical problem solving, that is the lane I like.

Can you build AI agents and workflow automation?

Yes. I can help design multi-step AI workflows, agent systems, intake logic, review processes, reporting flows, and automation that connects tools together instead of leaving everything in scattered tabs.

What is a RAG or knowledge-base system?

A RAG system connects AI responses to a trusted knowledge source. That makes answers more grounded, searchable, and useful for teams that need AI to work with real documentation, policies, records, or internal knowledge.

Can you help with AI image or video generation?

Yes. I work with AI image and video generation as part of studio and content workflows. That can mean concept development, repeatable creative systems, brand visuals, short-form content, or production pipelines that keep a style consistent.

Do you work with drone, ROV, or inspection workflows?

Yes. My technical background includes drone and ROV systems, troubleshooting, mission deployment, 3D printers, and field problem solving. That helps me think about AI systems in a way that respects the messy reality outside the screen.

What makes your background different from a typical AI developer?

I came through venue operations, fast-paced service work, engineering technician work, unmanned systems, 3D printing, AI media, and hands-on builds. I understand people, pressure, tools, and systems, which helps me bridge AI with business workflows that actually have to run.

Can you improve an existing workflow instead of building from scratch?

Absolutely. A smart project can start with the process you already have. From there, I can help clean up intake, automate repeat work, improve reporting, create AI review steps, or build a lightweight system around what is already working.

How do you approach security and reliability?

I care about system design before speed. That means scoped data access, clear boundaries, reviewable outputs, practical security thinking, and workflows that can be tested before anyone trusts them in a real environment.

How do I start a project with DigitalStoneAI Studio?

Start with the contact form and share the project type, the workflow, and what you want to improve. I can help turn the rough idea into a clear build path, whether it is a prototype, automation, AI system, or consulting conversation.
