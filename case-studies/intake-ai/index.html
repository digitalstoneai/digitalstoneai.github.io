Case Study Summary

Client
Glow Med Spa, Chula Vista, CA. Owner-operated med spa with 3 providers.
Challenge
Manual DMs, calls, and web forms caused an estimated 40% of after-hours inquiries to be lost.
System Type
Conversational AI intake, qualification scoring, booking automation, and SMS reactivation.
Primary Channels
Instagram DM, website form, SMS keyword, referral SMS.
Challenge

High-intent leads were arriving when nobody was watching

Appointment requests came through DMs, calls, and a contact form that was not monitored after 5pm. The owner needed a system that could respond quickly, qualify fit, book the right prospects, and keep lower-fit leads warm.

  • Lead loss: roughly 40% of after-hours inquiries were being lost to faster competitors.
  • Follow-up gap: no system existed for cold leads or post-consult no-shows.
  • Operational drag: staff time was being spent manually repeating the same qualification work.

Lead Intelligence

The automation was shaped around real timing patterns

  • Source mix: Instagram DM 51%, website form 28%, SMS keyword 21%.
  • Peak window: 34% of all leads arrived between 9pm and 11pm.
  • Qualification rate: 71% of leads were qualified for booking or VIP handoff.
  • Auto-booking: 58% of qualified leads booked automatically.
  • VIP handoff: 13% of qualified leads were flagged and texted to the owner directly.
  • Lower-fit handling: 29% were declined or routed into nurture.
