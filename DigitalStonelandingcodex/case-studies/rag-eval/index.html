Project Summary

Client
Coastal Legal Group, San Diego, CA. Mid-size personal injury law firm with 12 attorneys and 8 staff.
Challenge
A prior vendor delivered a chatbot with a 34% hallucination rate on factual case data.
System Type
Automated RAG benchmark, failure analysis, and remediation workflow.
Business Risk
Incorrect case facts could damage trust before the system ever reached clients.
Challenge

Preventing hallucination-prone answers from going live

The firm needed a RAG system that could answer intake questions from case documents. The inherited chatbot was returning invented or mismatched facts on high-stakes details like settlement demand amounts, hospital visits, and statute deadlines.

  • Primary problem: generated answers were not reliably grounded in retrieved documents.
  • Evaluation target: faithfulness, answer relevance, context precision, and failure cases.
  • Success condition: ship only after the retrieval pipeline could be measured and repaired.

Remediation

The fix was not just a better prompt

  • Chunking fix: switched from 512-token flat chunks to 1024-token chunks with 20% overlap and metadata tagging.
  • Evaluation fix: used RAGAS metrics for faithfulness, answer relevance, and context precision.
  • Pipeline fix: statute and date fields required a dedicated extraction agent instead of standard semantic retrieval.
  • Delivery fix: evaluation reports were automated through n8n so the client could review failures without digging through logs.
