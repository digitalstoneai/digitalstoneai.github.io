Project Summary

Client
The Meridian, San Diego, CA. Independent 280-capacity music venue and bar running 4-6 events per week.
Challenge
The owner had no visibility into repeat guests, revenue quality, or which events created loyal audiences.
System Type
Automated venue intelligence brief using POS, ticketing, check-in, and AI summary generation.
Delivery
Plain-English SMS brief every Monday at 8am, with one-tap campaign approval.
Challenge

Full rooms were hiding weak return signals

Booking decisions were made on gut feel and visible attendance. The owner could pack the room without knowing whether those guests returned, spent well, or built the kind of audience that made future events stronger.

  • Blind spot: attendance did not reveal repeat rate or revenue quality.
  • Data problem: POS, ticketing, and check-in signals lived in separate tools.
  • Workflow problem: the owner did not want another dashboard to manage.

Key Insight

The best-looking events were not the best business

High-attendance DJ and dance events had the lowest repeat rates, between 18% and 31%. Lower-attendance acoustic, jazz, and trivia events had the highest repeat rates, between 68% and 79%, plus stronger revenue per head.

  • Old behavior: optimize for room capacity and energy.
  • New behavior: balance attendance with return signal and spend quality.
  • Campaign trigger: owner could reply Y to approve reactivation messages for flagged guest segments.
